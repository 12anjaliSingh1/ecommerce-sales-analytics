# E-Commerce Sales & Customer Analytics Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce sales and customer data to understand revenue trends,
product performance, and geographic sales distribution. The goal is to support data-driven
business decision-making using interactive dashboards.

## Business Problem
The business needed a centralized analytics solution to:
- Monitor overall sales performance
- Identify top-performing product categories
- Track monthly revenue trends
- Understand geographic distribution of sales

## Tools Used
- Power BI (Data Modeling, DAX, Visualization)
- Excel (Data Cleaning)
- SQL (Data Analysis)
- Python (Exploratory Data Analysis)

## Key KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Orders
- Total Customers
- Monthly Revenue Trend

## Dashboard Features
- KPI cards for high-level performance tracking
- Monthly revenue trend analysis
- Top product categories by revenue
- State-wise sales distribution using map visualization
- Interactive slicers for Year and Customer State

## Business Insights
- Revenue showed steady growth from 2016 to early 2018
- A few product categories contributed the majority of revenue
- Sales were concentrated in specific high-performing states
